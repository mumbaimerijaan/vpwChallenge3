# Architectural Decisions

## Why a Cinematic Intro?
To foster empathy and explicitly satisfy the **Understand** requirement. A traditional form lacks the emotional weight needed to spur climate action. The GSAP scrolling experience builds context before asking for data.

## Why Personalized Recommendations?
Generic advice is often ignored. By directly linking the user's explicit choices (e.g., "Frequent Flying") to targeted actionable advice (e.g., "Reduce short-haul flights"), we satisfy the **Reduce** criteria with maximum practicality.

## Why LocalStorage Tracking?
To satisfy the **Track** requirement while maintaining a zero-trust, high-privacy profile. Users can measure their progress indefinitely without surrendering personal data to a centralized server.

## Why Awareness Scoring and Bands?
People respond to gamification and categorization. Assigning score bands ("Eco Explorer", "Climate Champion") provides clear milestones for improvement.
