# Privacy Policy

CarbonStory respects user privacy by operating completely independently of backend data collection architectures. 

## What data is stored?
We store only numerical scores, date strings, and categorized assessment values derived from the CarbonStory quiz. 

## Why it is stored
This data is stored solely to power the "Your Carbon Journey" historical tracking feature, allowing users to measure their improvement over time.

## How long it is stored
The data is stored indefinitely in your browser's local cache until manually cleared.

## Local browser only
All data processing and storage occur exclusively on your device. 

## No server-side storage
No database or backend server ever receives, processes, or logs your assessment results.

## No personal information
We do not ask for names, emails, locations, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

## User Control
The user maintains full ownership of their data. Users can clear all data instantly by clearing their browser's local storage or site data.
